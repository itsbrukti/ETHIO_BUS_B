About EthioGo

Revolutionizing bus travel in Ethiopia — one seat at a time

Who We Are

EthioGo is a modern digital platform that connects passengers directly with bus owners. We were founded with a simple mission: to eliminate the chaos of traditional bus travel in Ethiopia.

No more waking up at 4 AM to stand in long queues. No more confusion about seat availability. No more overbooking. Just simple, reliable, instant bus ticket booking.

The Problem We Solve

Tired of waking up at 4 AM to stand in long queues? Frustrated with confusion about seat availability? Worried about overbooking? We felt the same. That's why we built EthioGo.

Our Solution

A seamless digital experience where you can book your seat from anywhere, anytime. Bus owners can manage trips digitally, and passengers get instant confirmation.

Who Uses Our System

Passengers

Book seats from anywhere, anytime. Get instant confirmation and travel stress-free.

Bus Owners / Drivers

Manage trips, see passenger lists digitally, and prevent overbooking.

Admin

Control everything — approve buses, manage users, and monitor the system.
